Overview

XYG is a window-systems manufacturer. We rebuilt their brand identity and communications to translate technical precision into emotional warmth.

The Challenge

Industrial brands struggle to feel human. XYG's competitors looked like spec sheets. We needed an identity that signaled craftsmanship without losing engineering credibility.

Process

Workshops with the founder + ops team. Mood-board reviews. Three identity directions, narrowed to one over two presentation rounds. Applied across print, web, and packaging.
